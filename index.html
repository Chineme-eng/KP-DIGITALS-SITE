Frequently Asked Questions

Do I own my website once it's done?

+
Yes, 100%. Unlike some agencies that hold your site hostage, you own everything — the design, the content, and the domain. We don't lock you into any proprietary platforms.

How long does it take to build my site?

+
Most Starter and Growth sites are designed, revised, and launched within 5–7 business days. Larger projects (like e-commerce) take 10–14 days. We move fast.

What if I need changes after the site is live?

+
We offer an optional $45/mo "Peace of Mind" maintenance plan that covers premium hosting, daily backups, and minor text/image updates. Or, you can request changes à la carte for a small flat fee.

Do I need to provide content and photos?

+
If you have photos and text, great! If not, don't worry. We can source high-quality, professional stock photography for your industry and help write compelling copy that converts visitors into customers.

How do payments work?

+
We take a 50% deposit to begin the project and secure your spot in our schedule. The final 50% is only paid right before we push your finished website live. We accept all major cards, Zelle, and Apple Pay.
