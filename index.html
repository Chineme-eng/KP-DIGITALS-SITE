Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we start.

Do I own the website once it's built? +
Yes, absolutely. Once the final payment is made, you own 100% of the website, the design, and all its content. We don't hold your site hostage.
Are there any hidden monthly fees? +
No. The prices you see above are one-time fees for the design and build. You will only need to pay for your own hosting and domain name (usually around $10-$20/month depending on the provider). We can also manage hosting for you if you prefer!
What if I need changes after it goes live? +
We include unlimited revisions *during* the build process. After launch, we offer affordable maintenance plans if you want us to handle ongoing updates, or we can train you to make simple text/image swaps yourself.
